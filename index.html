FAQ

How will I benefit from this course?
By taking this course, you'll acquire practical automation skills that are highly sought after in the tech industry. These skills will enable you to work on real-world projects, enhance your resume, and open up new career opportunities.

After completing the training, can I secure a job?
Yes, our course is designed to help you land a job. We offer comprehensive interview preparation, CV enhancement tips, and access to our network of hiring partners to increase your chances of securing a position in automation.

Can I work remotely after finishing the course?
Absolutely! The skills you learn can be applied to remote positions, giving you the flexibility to work from anywhere in the world.

What prerequisites do I need to join this course?
This course is suitable for learners at all levels, from beginners to advanced. No prior experience in automation is required, although a basic understanding of programming concepts can be helpful.

How long does the course take to complete?
The course is designed to be completed in 8-12 weeks, depending on your pace. We offer flexible learning schedules to fit your availability and learning speed.

What kind of projects will I work on during the course?
You will engage in real-world projects such as developing test frameworks, performing API testing, and setting up CI/CD pipelines. These hands-on experiences are designed to equip you with practical skills that you can showcase to potential employers.

How do I know if this course is right for me?
This course is ideal if you're looking to start or advance a career in automation. It's perfect for those who want hands-on experience with real projects and seek a flexible learning environment that caters to both beginners and experienced professionals.

What do I need to participate in the course?
You'll need a computer with internet access to participate in the course. All necessary software and tools will be provided or guided through the course.
